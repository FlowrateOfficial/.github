## What we do

FlowRate helps you **connect banks once**, **switch financial context in a tap**, and **see what matters** — without living in spreadsheets.

| Space | For |
|:-----:|:----|
| 🧑 **Independent** | Personal budgets and solo finances |
| 🏠 **Household** | Shared expenses and fair splits |
| 👨‍👩‍👧 **Family** | Allowances, invites, supervised teen views |
| 🏢 **Company** | Burn rate, runway, and business cash flow |

### Highlights

- 🔗 **Bank linking** — Plaid (EU) & Stripe Financial Connections (US)
- 📊 **Money view** — accounts, transactions, budgets, analytics
- 🛡️ **SaaS Shield** — catch subscription creep and duplicates
- 💳 **Pro billing** — Stripe Checkout & customer portal
- 💬 **In-app feedback** — user reports flow into private GitHub issues
- 🌍 **i18n** — English (US/UK) & French
